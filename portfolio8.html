Kristy Dahlquist, PMP

I’m a full-stack problem-solver with a thing for structure, strategy, and sandwiches (in no particular order). I’ve led marketing and program management efforts at customer-driven brands like REI, Okta, and Auth0—connecting developers, communities, and customers in ways that keeps things human.

I build programs, workflows, and experiences that make teams more effective and ideas more doable. I also love speaking about overcoming fear, building habits, community building, and the occasional misadventure. Whether I’m launching an initiative or untangling a messy system, I bring order to chaos without killing the creative energy.

If you’re looking for someone who brings energy, adaptability, and a people-first, data-driven approach to every challenge, let’s connect on LinkedIn!
